why choose us

our passion.

Southwall Studios started as the culmination of three friends who loved photography and flying drones around all day. When we figured we could make our living off of it, we made the leap to strive for excellence in our service.

our process.

The model for our success has been based on listening to what our clients want. Exemplary, hassle-free solutions are custom-tailored to suit the needs of our clientele.

our results.

We treat our services as a craft. Our photos and videos are held to the highest standards of scrutiny by our image specialists and photography team. With each shoot, we aim to create an exceptional visual representation for your project.

the guarantee

Should you feel that the media you receive does not hold to the standards of excellence, due to the fault of the photographer and editing team, then we will work to fix the problem. However, if at the end of the day, our efforts to deliver fall below your expectations, we offer a complete refund.

At Southwall Studios, our foundations are in the relationships we build and the quality of our work. Listening to and creating relationships with each one of our clients has been an essential part to the Southwall model.
